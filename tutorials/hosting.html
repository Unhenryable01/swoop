How To Host A Server?

Introduction

To host a Swoop Server, you need a Github/Gitlab account, a website that allows you to connect your Github/Gitlab repositorys too and a brain.

How To Host Then?

Well, it's easy! Just fork it and connect the repository to your website! I use Cloudflare's pages. It will do all the work itself, and once it's deployed, it's done! Spread the word about your server and tweak it however you want! Just keep it free and open source, as if you don't then we will have to report your site and take it down for copyright!
